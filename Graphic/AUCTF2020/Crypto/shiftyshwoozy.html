AUCTF 2020 - Crypto - Shifty Shwoozy

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Shifty Shwoozy
Prompt: Eve woke up to this message after a really crazy night. Everything is shifted around. Can you figure out what it says?
You can download the message here:
https://drive.google.com/file/d/1hshUoTtN5tgVjbpRNDujmBnKOJbJZcbS/view?usp=sharing
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

When I downloaded that file, I immediately thought we were dealing with a vigenere cipher.
Personally, I use a site that works very well for the figure of vigenere: https://www.mygeocachingprofile.com/codebreaker.vigenerecipher.aspx.
So we enter a part of the ciphered text inside, we wait a few minutes, and the encryption key comes out: COINCIDENCESMAYHELPYOUSOLVETHISPROBLEM.
So we decipher all the text with this key.
In the middle of the text, which is an excerpt from The Great Gaby, we notice: sometimes you have to do things yourself.
Replace the spaces with underscores and wrap it in the usual format.
So we have our flag: auctf{sometimes_you_have_to_do_things_yourself}
